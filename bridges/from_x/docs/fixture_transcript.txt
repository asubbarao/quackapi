--- observed gap (matches docs/fromfast.md gap analysis) ---
POST /api/login carries no BODY SCHEMA because the fixture handler binds the
body implicitly (`def login(user: UserInLogin)`), not via explicit
`Body(..., embed=True)`. The driver only emits BODY SCHEMA for the explicit-
Body pattern proven on fastapi-realworld; implicit Pydantic-typed parameters
are a known, documented gap, not a silent failure — the route still registers
and returns 201, just without body validation.
